    Aunak Company for Smart Education Technology

    Aunak is an education company duly licensed by the Dubai Integrated Economic Zones Authority under license number 13329, issued on 23/1/2022. Our mission is to innovate and deliver effective solutions that elevate the standards of education across all sectors.

    Our Goals

    To develop practical skills based on advanced scientific methodologies and cutting-edge technologies, thereby enhancing the quality of education.

    To foster innovation in the realm of technical and digital education.

    To promote academic and cultural exchange on a global scale, emphasizing the importance of international experiences, particularly in Japan.

    To strengthen community engagement through various initiatives that enhance employability skills in today's dynamic job market.

    To invest in human capital and develop a new generation of leaders who can shape the future.

    Our Features

    Extensive Experience

    We are proud of our creative team that keeps pace with the latest developments in education and technology.

    Commitment to Quality

    We undertake the provision of high-quality services that meet and exceed expectations.

    Comprehensive Services

    We meet innovative educational and technological needs on a wide scale in all fields of education.

    Distinguished cadres

    Our team includes a select group of qualified teachers trained by global experts using the latest teaching methods, in addition to providing continuous programs for them to develop their skills and knowledge of the latest educational technology methods. We also conduct continuous evaluation of their educational performance to be compatible with global standards

    Geographic Spread

    We provide our services through our branches spread across various countries of the world.

    Competitive Prices

    Investments are studied to suit the budget of our customers.
