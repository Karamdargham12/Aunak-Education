    Our Partners

    The Syrian Virtual Smart School

    This is the first virtual school officially licensed by the Ministry of Education in the Syrian Arab Republic and the first educational institution to obtain the international ISO 2018_21001 certificate.
    It includes thousands of students spread across 35 countries around the world. It is concerned with teaching the Syrian curriculum from the elementary stage
    until the end of secondary education in both the scientific and literary branches, using modern and advanced methods, and under the supervision of a distinguished cadre
    It also adheres to granting officially certified certificates accredited by the Syrian Ministry of Education.
    This is based on the belief in the importance of continuous education and providing learning opportunities for all, regardless of the surrounding circumstances

    The Syrian Virtual University

    It is a higher educational institution in Syria that aims to provide higher education opportunities for all, regardless of place or circumstances. The university relies on the latest technologies in the field of distance education to provide its academic programs, allowing students to study and learn flexibly and easily

    The Arab Network for Creativity and Innovation

    It seeks to enhance creativity and innovation in the Arab world by supporting and incubating distinguished youth initiatives for Arab creators and innovators. It also aims to provide a civilizational service that enhances cooperation and integration between visions and ideas, and coordinates efforts between the public and private sectors

    The Prestigious Chukyo University

    This university was established in 1954 AD, and its permanent slogan is "seriousness," and its founding spirit is "the quality of higher education in the academic and mathematical fields." This university is committed to keeping pace with modern development and managing the future, so that students can enjoy challenges to the fullest, create new values, and flourish in the global community.

    Liebe Japanese Company

    It is a company specializing in programs for developing the capacities of teachers and specialists in the field of early childhood and education, as it provides a specialized curriculum for developing the physical, mental, and health senses for the early childhood stage, in addition to enhancing children's thinking and creativity skills through the three essential principles (parallel play – cognitive skills – social skills), which provides early childhood support programs starting from the age of 4 years through

    1

    Giving children new horizons of learning and creativity

    2

    Stimulating curiosity, freedom of expression, and exploration

    3

    Educational curricula that support the creative sense in modern ways

    4

    Specialized Japanese programs for developing the child's formation from the mental, physical, academic, and social aspects under the supervision of Japanese experts

    5

    Innovative programs for providing psychological and social treatment for children

    6

    Integrating children with special needs with students through rehabilitation and recreational programs

    7

    Providing psychological and emotional support

    TANQ Arab Japanese Academy

    It is an academy concerned with providing many programs for children from 5 to 8 years old, aiming through them to enhance positive participation, freedom of expression, thinking and research, discovering information and enhancing hobbies through sustainable stages, and among these programs are:

    Creative programs

    Aim to establish children in mental, physical, and creative skills

    Exploratory programs

    Work on discovering the world around children through research, observation, and experimentation

    Outputs and cooperation

    Aim to share creative activities with Japanese friends.

    Mint Institute for Phonetics Learning

    It is an advanced educational program for teaching English and phonetics in scientific, visual, and auditory ways that are interesting to facilitate teaching methods for teachers, as well as developing self-learning methods for students at all ages and students who have difficulty learning.
