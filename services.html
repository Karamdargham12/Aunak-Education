    services

    Cognitive Skills Development

    We provide high-quality programs and courses that contribute to the development of cognitive skills and thinking skills for different age groups.

    E-learning

    We offer a wide range of tools and technologies that support online learning, as well as interactive digital educational content that ensures a comprehensive and flexible academic learning experience that meets the needs of learners at any time.

    Instructor-led training open class

    Teacher training in a classroom through a comprehensive experience that enhances the trainee's ability to take full advantage of training programs through lectures and interactive workshops in the presence of colleagues and under the guidance of a professional trainer.

    Webinars

    Webinars are live or recorded presentations that staff can view at their convenience, presented by the trainer at intervals.

    On-the-job training

    This type of training takes place in the workplace, or in a special training environment, with the aim of improving the skills that are directly implemented in the actual work environment, improving productivity and efficiency, in addition to regularly evaluating the performance of trainees and providing ongoing support and motivation.

    Training teachers on introducing technology and artificial intelligence programs into education

    The teacher is considered the first source of inspiration for the student, from whom the student derives morals, values, and ways of thinking. Therefore, the teacher must qualify the student to use technology by integrating educational videos and interactive programs into the educational process, and entering interactive digital educational content and preparing it, and encouraging the student to self-learn and how to use reliable sources of information, and using technology responsibly to enhance his ability to self-learn, manage time, and strengthen his creative thinking in education.

    Gamification

    It is an educational approach that combines learning and fun through activities and games, which helps to enhance the learning experience and increase understanding and comprehension using games and interactive activities that stimulate creativity and innovation and enhance memory and understanding.

    Student-centered learning

    This mechanism aims to engage children in the educational process through a set of activities and skills provided by teachers and specialists, making the learning environment more stimulating and enjoyable for students, and also contributing to conveying information in an innovative mental style. Some of the most important programs dedicated to it are:

    1

    This model focuses on students learning through designing and implementing practical projects, where students collaborate and work as a team to solve a specific problem or produce a specific product. This model requires students to be active in the learning process and to use analysis, critical thinking, and innovation skills. Among these projects are what are known as "virtual projects", such as:
    • Power Point, Word, Adobe, Imove, Movie Maker
    • Stories and plays
    • Video challenges
    • Educational games
    • Stories and animations
    • Research, presentations, and social activities

    2

    Cooperative Learning: This model focuses on encouraging cooperation and interaction between students. Students work together in small groups, exchanging knowledge and observations to solve a problem. This model enhances communication skills, teamwork, and the ability to work in diverse teams.

    3

    Individual Cooperative Learning: This model combines cooperation and individual work, where students sometimes work individually on specific tasks, and at other times collaborate with each other to exchange knowledge, ideas, and provide feedback. This model helps students develop their individual skills and benefit from the experiences of their peers at the same time.

    4

    Discovery-Based Learning: This model relies on encouraging students to explore concepts and ideas on their own by asking questions, researching, and investigating. Students develop research, inference, and critical thinking skills to solve a problem.

    Support programs designed specifically for students enrolled in virtual education (students in virtual schools and universities)

    Programs for Virtual School Students:

    At Aunak, we believe in the end of the traditional education era, and the need for the labor market for higher thinking skills that rely on analysis, innovation, and evaluation, to keep pace with technology and artificial intelligence programs on an ongoing basis. Therefore, we offer a set of educational, cultural, and recreational programs that open new horizons for learning and creativity, designed specifically to meet the needs of virtual education students, following a new pattern that pushes the student towards critical thinking and suits all ages from the preparatory stages (preschool) until the end of secondary education and in all subjects. These programs support the student's creative sense in modern ways with the aim of providing a comprehensive learning experience that combines education, benefit, and enjoyment, which enhances student interaction and increases their understanding and comprehension of information, through an educational network that helps teachers and students communicate and exchange educational resources, and conduct tests and discussions. To contribute to the development of critical thinking, creativity, problem-solving skills, stimulating curiosity and freedom of expression, supporting students' emotional and social skills, and consolidating their academic achievement, in addition to focusing heavily on ethics and values as the foundation of building a human being. These programs include:

    1

    Preparing the student to deal with advanced technology in conjunction with establishing a culture of innovation and creativity for him.

    2

    Interactive educational activities: aimed at enhancing critical thinking skills to stimulate curiosity and exploration among them, solve problems for students in an innovative way, and develop their cooperation and communication skills.

    3

    Rich cultural events: introduce students to ancient world cultures and enrich their perceptions of different world civilizations to ensure that they receive a modern integrated education.

    4

    Innovative recreational programs: provide students with the opportunity to enjoy fun and motivating learning experiences outside the framework of traditional curricula. To build a student who is academically, mentally, and socially balanced.

    5

    Attention to mental health: as it is an important part of achieving academic and personal success for students, through programs dedicated to awareness and social education and providing psychological support and counseling, which helps students talk about their psychological and emotional problems in a safe and supportive environment and under the supervision of a specialist.

    Programs offered to university students

    Societies today are measured by the ability of human capital to innovate, so we at Aunak were keen to prepare training and qualification programs specifically for virtual and traditional university students so that students can acquire complete knowledge and skills that qualify them to enter the changing labor market and introduce the applied and research aspects to traditional education.

    Pre-graduation university student programs:

    1

    Holding many academic and international forums in Arab universities, through taking advantage of Aunak's international partnerships.

    2

    Opening up horizons for academic and research cooperation between international universities and Arab universities.

    3

    Attracting modern technological programs applied in international universities, and integrating them into the programs of Arab virtual and traditional universities.

    4

    Qualifying and empowering graduate students and providing global expertise to master the tools of future management.

    5

    Qualifying and guiding students who have received scholarships to study abroad by guiding them academically and culturally, and providing personal, social, academic, and linguistic support by offering courses to improve English language skills or the language of the country.

    6

    Holding international academic forums within Arab universities to build bridges of cooperation, mutual understanding, and fruitful cooperation that contributes to enhancing scientific innovation in several fields.

    International Awareness Programs

    The program seeks to enhance international awareness through training and empowerment to participate effectively in diverse linguistic and cultural environments through programs offered to students traveling to new countries, to be directed to many important matters, such as the language of the country, its cultural diversity, and its customs and traditions.

    Montessori

    We are inspired by the Montessori curriculum at Aunak Education to prefer educational methods that include children's natural interests and activities instead of formal teaching methods, and we provide a stimulating learning environment that allows them to explore new ideas, experiment with problem-solving, and develop innovative projects that contribute to solving community challenges, to be independent learners able to discover their interests and develop their knowledge independently.

    Providing education for people of determination

    We at Aunak for Smart Education Technology firmly believe that all students, including those with disabilities, form a cornerstone for the success of the educational process. We also strive to build an inclusive and supportive learning environment where all teachers, students, and parents work together to ensure that every individual has equal opportunities to learn and grow.

    Training teachers to deal with programs for people of determination

    Developing and implementing these programs requires the concerted efforts of teachers, psychologists, and social workers to ensure the provision of a supportive and integrated learning environment for students with special needs. Therefore, Aunak Education seeks to provide specialized teachers trained with global academic training to deal with all the required needs.

    Shadow teachers

    Shadow teacher programs are educational initiatives aimed at providing intensive individual support to students who need additional help to get the most out of the learning process. He accompanies him in his classroom and provides him with the necessary support and guidance.

    Support programs for students with disabilities include

    Individual educational programs

    Individual educational programs are developed by a multidisciplinary team and include specific educational goals, required support services, and appropriate assessment methods

    Inclusion programs

    Inclusion programs aim to integrate students with disabilities into regular classrooms while providing additional support. This support may include a shadow teacher, a teaching assistant, or modifications to the curriculum.

    Special classes

    These classes are designed for students who need a separate learning environment due to their special needs.

    Early intervention programs

    These programs target young children (from birth to 5 years) who experience developmental delays or disabilities.

    Vocational and technical education

    These programs aim to prepare students with disabilities to enter the workforce. They include training in specific vocational and technical skills, and cooperation with external parties to provide practical training opportunities

    Technological support programs

    Using assistive technology to enable students to learn in a way that suits their specific needs. These tools may include specially designed computer programs, or assistive devices such as tablets or alternative communication devices.

    Psychological and behavioral support programs

    These programs provide psychological and behavioral support to students with psychological or behavioral disorders. This support is provided through individual or group sessions with specialists
